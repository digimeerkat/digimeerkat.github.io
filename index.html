Vision of digimeerkat.com

Our vision is to combine talent, experience and technology to create a value added service that is in reach of every business.

The aim is to assist businesses to monitor and analyze those “out of sight” risks and prevent unexpected expenditure when things go wrong.

We believe in keeping things “simple”, ensuring stability without the burden of a large capital outlay.

Services

Our product offering is an independent system that gathers information from various sensors, sending data to a central hosted server and making current and historic information securely available to you where-ever you are.

Examples of typical parameters that we measure (although not limited to) are machine motion status, power consumption, temperature, movement, product count, general liquid and fuel levels, etc.
